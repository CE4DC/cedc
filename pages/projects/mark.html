Marketplace

Mission statement:

To develop a safe and functioning marketplace in Cange, Haiti.

Project description:

The current marketplace in Cange, Haiti runs into the streets, endangering travelers and customers, and has little room for expansion, limiting the local economy. Marketplace aims to develop a new marketplace, stimulating the economy in Cange and the surrounding areas and to offer a comprehensive proposal of the marketplace including the materials, costs, schedule, and location of the future marketplace to the city of Cange, Haiti.
