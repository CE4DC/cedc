Waste Management

Mission statement:

To design a waste management system that empowers the local people to achieve sustainable waste disposal while benefiting both the sanitation and environmental quality of Cange.

Project description:

In Cange, Haiti there is no system for managing trash. Trash is discarded anywhere, collecting and posing serious environmental and santiational issues, and it is a social norm to just throw trash anywhere. Waste Management's goal is to educate the people in Cange about waste management while also providing a facility to help manage waste.
