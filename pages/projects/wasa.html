Water System Analysis

Mission statement:

To upgrade infrastructure by integrating a water-testing laboratory and other technologies through the use of new standard operating procedures.

Project description:

Water Systems aims to constantly monitor the water system for any issues of safety and quality of the drinking water that reaches citizens in Cange, Haiti. They will achieve this goal by writing a recommendation report based on analyzed water systems data and integrating a water-testing laboratory inside of the pump unit.
