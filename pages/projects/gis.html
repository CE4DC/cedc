Global Information Systems

Mission statement:

GIS's mission is to providing mapping and GIS support for CEDC and creating a web-accessible database for easy access by other groups.

Project description:

GIS is comprised of four groups: GIS Mapping which handles requests, Web Mapping which is creating a website with GIS information, an NGO Fusion Cell which tracks NGOs and the work they are doing in Haiti, and the Energy Credits group which encourages alternative energy and sustainable practices.
