Irrigation

Mission statement:

To promote economic development in Cange, Haiti, to create sustainable solutions involving agriculture, and to deliver an irrigation system to the farms in and around the community of Cange.

Project description:

The farmers in Haiti must endure a 4-5 month dry season every year that destroys their primary source of income for half of the year. Irrigation is address this problem and it's goals are to to improve crop yields, boost harvests, and increase efficiency of the farms in Cange by creating irrigation systems.
