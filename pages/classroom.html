CEDC began in the classroom. And the class element remains an integral part of the program. Averaging between 60-80 students per semester, with 25+ majors represented, CEDC offers students college credit for participating in the organization. Through multi-discleplinary high performance teams, guest speakers, numerous presentations, and networking, CEDC's classroom experience develops students in both their technical skills and soft skills.
